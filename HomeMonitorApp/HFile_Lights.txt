1. Select the Light from the list of lights
2. Color wheel will change color for the light.
	-click w in the center for white color
3. On/Off buttons will turn light on or off.
4. Brightness slider moves up and down to change brightness.


	To Add a Light
1.click add device
2.Enter the info for that device in the pop up dialog
3.Enter OK to save light or cancel.
